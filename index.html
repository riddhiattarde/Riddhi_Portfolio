So, who am I?

I’m Riddhi Attarde, a third-year Electrical & Computer Engineering student. I’m the person who stays up chasing random ideas just to see what sticks, there's something addictive about that "what if we tried this instead?" moment.

When I’m not buried in circuits, I’m building communities. As the Soceity of Women Engineers Asian Connections AG Lead-Elect, I work to make sure women actually feel at home in engineering.

The Digital Footprint

I don't just build hardware; I build audiences. I manage social accounts with a massive 10 million+ monthly reach (hitting 1.9 million on Pinterest alone last year). I also review advanced copies of dark thrillers and murder mysteries, basically, I'm obsessed with how complex systems, and stories, work.
